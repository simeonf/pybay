Event Focus

Stay relevant

It is so easy to get content and become obsolete as technology advances.

That’s why PyBay brings together the most impactful technologies and influential speakers today so you can get up-to-date in the shortest amount of time. Whether you are interested in web technologies, data, devops, python internals or performance, whether you are just starting out or a seasoned developer, we got you covered to stay on top of the game.

And if you are in SF/Bay Area, save big on travel cost as the experts come to us. Hurray!

Get brilliant

There is a shortage of skills and people with great attitudes. Be the solution.

In addition to 40 talks and 20 Lightning talks, PyBay 2017 is adding 8 pre-conference workshops (full-day and half day) for those who want to go the extra mile to dive deeper. Better yet, share what you’ve learned to cement the knowledge or impress upon the sponsors that are looking to hire during the main conference days.

Pray that a mutant from Xavier appears or invest in yourself and your staff.

Code together, laugh together

Aside from the talks and the food, the hallway track was the most loved feature at PyBay 2016.

Imagine yourself diving deep with the luminaries or ‘former' username, coding with a new buddy or mentoring someone at Hacker Lounge because you are inspired, sharing your passion project at Open Spaces, refreshing your mind and body with yoga, laughing out loud at improv, playing some tunes under the sun…

The Python Community is great, we dare you to make it even better!
