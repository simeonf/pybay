Ticket Types

Corporate Ticket

We love companies that support employes' continual education.  Therefore, if your company sends >20% of the engineering team OR >10 developers to PyBay with the corporate rate, we'll feature your company's logo among the Fans of Python.

Individual Ticket

Your employer is not reimbursing your ticket?  On a tight budget or in-between jobs?  A student? We've got you covered with individual tickets. However, please get your ticket ASAP - prices go up as the allotted tickets at each price tier sell out.

Pre-Conference Workshop Only

Want to add to your conference by deep diving with some of the top educators in the python ecosystems?  Or skip the conference and just take the pre-conference workshops?  Tickets for full-day a half-day work shops are available on an a la carte bases.

One-day ticket

Have too much going on but still want to join in on the fun to geek out with other Pythonistas and attend some talks?   Come on Saturday or Sunday, your call.  Make it happen.

Work-trade Ticket

Want trade your talents in for a discount? We have 20 work-trade slots during the conference where you pay only 1/3 the lowest price, and we have roles handling pre-conference tasks where we will comp your ticket.  See what help we need and apply away!
